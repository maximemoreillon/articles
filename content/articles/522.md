I got myself a fume extractor but it takes a rather large amount of real-estate on my desk. I decided to mount it on an arm so that I could easily move it out of the way so I designed and 3D printed an attachment bracket for it.

![](https://img.maximemoreillon.com/images/614fd10f9a075f99a459c899)The bracket was designed in Fusion 360

![](https://img.maximemoreillon.com/images/614fd8ca9a075faaea59c8ad)And then printed on my QIDI Tech 1

![](https://img.maximemoreillon.com/images/614fd97c9a075f778d59c8b1)It ended up fitting the fume extractor nicely

![](https://img.maximemoreillon.com/images/614fd99b9a075f1d4c59c8b5)The STL file for this part is available on [Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5145169)
