Items to which `v-if` or `v-for` apply can be grouped inside `<template>` tags. For example:

```
<ul>
  <template v-for="item in items">
    <li>{{ item.msg }}</li>
    <li class="divider" role="presentation"></li>
  </template>
</ul>
```
